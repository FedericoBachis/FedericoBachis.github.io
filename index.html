About Me

Indie Game Developer and Unity Developer since 2017, I specialize in 3D environment development and gamification for research applications. Currently, I work as a Research Technologist in Game-Based Interaction Systems at CRS4, where I contribute to cultural heritage projects, digital twins, and immersive VR experiences. I hold a degree in Computer Science and have extensive experience in scientific writing, patent documentation, and technical project support.

Sarim

An immersive VR experience designed for HTC Vive, showcasing the rich cultural heritage of Sardinia. Using photogrammetry techniques, the project recreates high-fidelity 3D models of significant Sardinian monuments, allowing users to explore historical sites in a virtual environment.
